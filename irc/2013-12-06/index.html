21:32 <aaronpk> oeyvind: I think I know the problem
21:32 <aaronpk> I just checked the triggers for your layer
21:33 <aaronpk> the radius on them is ridiculously tiny. You have radius values from 0.002 to a max of 5.
21:34 <aaronpk> this is in meters, which means your smallest trigger is 2 millimeters
21:35 <aaronpk> in your curl examples, you're sending a location update at the exact location of the center of the trigger, so it fires no problem
21:35 <aaronpk> but the phones of course send other values, and even if they're really close, they're not within 2mm of your triggers
21:36 <aaronpk> so, the solution is basically to make your triggers larger. we recommend a minimum of 50 meters in realtime/fine mode.
