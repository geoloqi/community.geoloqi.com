    Yes, this is certainly possible. Most of our sample apps work this way. Please see the DinoDeals app as a reference. https://github.com/geoloqi/DinoDeals

    This app has a small web server which creates the triggers, and the phone simply subscribes to the layer that has those triggers. I believe this is what you are looking for.
